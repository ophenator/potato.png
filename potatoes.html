Potatoes?

One question Potato.png often gets is: How many potatoes are there?

While we don't have an exact number, we do know that potatoes are one of the most widely grown root vegetables in the world.

Here is a table comparing and contrasting 6 common types of potatoes:

Type Color Texture Best Uses
Russet Brown Fluffy Baking, frying, mashing
Red Red Waxy Boiling, roasting, salads
Yukon Gold Yellow Creamy Mashing, roasting, soups
Fingerling Varies (yellow, red, purple) Firm and waxy Roasting, boiling, salads
Purple Peruvian Purple Dense and waxy Boiling, roasting, salads
